Lasgana

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese. It is typically baked in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Lasagna can be made with a variety of different ingredients, but the most common is ground beef. Other popular ingredients include sausage, meatballs, or vegetables. Lasagna is a hearty and satisfying dish that is perfect for a special occasion or a casual get-together.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15-ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (15-ounce) jar pasta sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 (15-ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 (10-ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 9 lasagna noodles

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large saucepan, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain off any excess fat.
  3. Add the onion and garlic to the saucepan and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  5. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, and 1/2 cup of the mozzarella cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Top with a layer of lasagna noodles, breaking them to fit as needed.
  8. Spread with half of the ricotta cheese mixture.
  9. Top with another layer of lasagna noodles.
  10. Spread with the remaining meat sauce.
  11. Top with the remaining ricotta cheese mixture.
  12. Sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella cheese.
  13. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  14. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  15. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
